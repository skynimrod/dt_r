.   1. 选择 CRAN Mirror

     "Packages" -> "Set CRAN mirror..."  , 选择 "China (Beijing 2)", 然后点击 "OK"

  2. "Packages" -> "Install Packgaes...", 选择 "ggplot2", 然后点击"OK"
 
     就会下载 ggplot2, 同时也会下载ggplot2 需要的其他包
